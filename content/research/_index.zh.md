Such smart interfaces enable real-time sensing, decoding, and modulation of neural activity, supporting unprecedented approaches for neurological diagnosis, functional restoration, and human-machine interaction, while significantly improving the precision, adaptability, and clinical potential of brain-machine technologies.

        <h2 class="text-3xl font-bold my-12">Micro-Nanorobots for Biomedical Therapy</h2>
        <div class="grid md:grid-cols-2 gap-12 items-center">
          <div>
            <img src="/images/research/microrobot.png" alt="Micro-Nanorobots" class="w-full h-auto">
          </div>
          <div class="text-lg leading-relaxed">
            With the rapid development of microfabrication and active matter research, micro-nanorobots can be precisely navigated inside complex biological environments. These tiny microscale machines achieve targeted drug delivery, localized tumor ablation, and minimally invasive microscale surgery. They open new technical avenues for precision disease treatment, greatly boosting targeting efficiency and biosafety of next-generation biomedical therapy.
          </div>
        </div>

        <h2 class="text-3xl font-bold my-12">Flexible Bioelectronic Devices</h2>
        <div class="grid md:grid-cols-2 gap-12 items-center">
          <div>
            <img src="/images/research/bioelectronics.png" alt="Flexible Bioelectronics" class="w-full h-auto">
          </div>
          <div class="text-lg leading-relaxed">
            Benefiting from soft functional materials and flexible manufacturing techniques, conformable bioelectronics can tightly attach to curved biological tissues. These flexible devices realize long-term stable monitoring of electrophysiological signals and physiological parameters, effectively reducing mechanical mismatch between rigid electronics and soft organs, and laying a solid foundation for wearable and implantable medical electronics.
          </div>
        </div>

        <h2 class="text-3xl font-bold my-12">Magnetorheological Smart Materials</h2>
        <div class="grid md:grid-cols-2 gap-12 items-center">
          <div>
            <img src="/images/research/magneto.png" alt="Magnetorheological Materials" class="w-full h-auto">
          </div>
          <div class="text-lg leading-relaxed">
            Driven by external magnetic fields, magnetorheological materials exhibit rapid and reversible changes in mechanical properties. We design composite magnetoactive materials with tunable stiffness and deformation behaviors, which are widely applied in soft actuator design, intelligent micro-manipulation, and adaptive robotic systems with fast magnetic response.
          </div>
        </div>
